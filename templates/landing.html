About Our Website

This platform is designed to support transparent and accessible civic participation. Citizens can engage in elections, submit requests, raise complaints, and stay updated through notifications. Admins can manage elections, monitor records, and support users.

Digital Voting

Citizens can securely participate in elections through token-based voting.

Service Requests

Users can submit civic service requests and track updates.

Complaints

Citizens can file complaints and receive administrative responses.

Notifications & Results

Stay informed about election updates, closures, and published results.
